Welcome to Kairosview

It is our goal to provide the citizens of Southeastern Idaho with up to the minute weather updates. The weather in our little slice of heaven is prone to change at the drop of a hat. You can count on us to give you and your family the most accurate forcast, so that you can plan ahead.

In addition to weather forcasts, we also provide you with important real time Coronavirus information for the state of Idaho.

Part of the unique experience of living in Southeastern Idaho is our spectacular views and many historical locations. With Kairosview you can select a specific location and get a complete weather summary about the area.
