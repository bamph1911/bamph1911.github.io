Target Audience:

A Description of the Target Audience

Kairosview's main audience are people who live near Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven Idaho. These rural communities experience a broad range of weather extremes throughout the year. Our target audience are users who want to make decisions based on our weather forcasts. Judging from the demographics of the towns mentioned. Farmers and ranchers could use our site to plan their work for the comming week.

Persona 1

The "Stay at Home Mom". The demographics from the community, show that the local woman's number one occupation is that of homemakers. This means that the mother decides and plans activities for her children to do throughout the day. They will use this site in order to get a good idea on how to dress herself and her children, the activites that they will do outdoors, and any accessories that they will need to bring with them.

Persona 2

The "Weekend Warrior". Like most rural communities, people spend a good portion of freetime outdoors. Camping, fishing, sports, and family activities are common activites for a typical Friday night. We need to build our site so that it is functional, easy to use, and updates the weather regularly

Scenario 1

Bill Bowers (age 67) is a longtime rancher. His livestock consist of mostly angus beef. He wants to know the likelyhood of the coming rainstorm's chance of lightning. He wants to know if he needs to seperate his cattle's hay. In order to avoid a possible fire, if it was struck by lightning.

Scenario 2

Amy Lines (age 33) Is a stay at home mom who is trying to keep her kids busy. The summer weather has been brutal this year and she is deciding if she wants to take her kids swimming on Saturday.

Scenario 3

Eric Banister (age 14) Normally has to walk to school in the mornings. Judging from the looks of it outside, this winter morning looks like a blizzard is on its way. He is trying to decide if he should walk or try to find a ride to school.

Scenario 4

Silvia Styles (age 78). Silvia's arthritis is acting up. She wants to check the barometer pressure to see if it is because of the weather or her new medication

Scenario 5

Brigham Smith (age 46) is the Boy Scout leader for his congregation's 11 year old scout group. They are planning a over night fishing trip near Fish Haven. The weather has been unpredictable and he is trying to decide if they will continue as planned or cancel for a later date.
